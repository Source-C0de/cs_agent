# SOP-PFAS-001 — PFAS Sampling in Drinking Water

Owner: Dr. Lena Park
Effective: 2026-01-15

## Scope
This SOP covers sample collection, container requirements, preservation, and
holding times for Per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) analysis in
drinking water and groundwater matrices by EPA Method 537.1.

## Container
- **1 × 250 mL HDPE bottle** with polypropylene cap (no PTFE liners).
- Bottles must be pre-rinsed with methanol and deionized water by the lab
  prior to shipment.

## Preservation
- Cool sample to **≤ 6 °C** but do NOT freeze.
- Add Trizma (or equivalent) preservative to pH 6.5–7.5 if provided by the lab
  in pre-dosed bottles.
- Hold at ≤ 6 °C from collection through receipt.

## Holding time
- Extract within **14 days** of collection.
- Analyze extract within **28 days** of extraction.

## Sample volume
- Minimum 250 mL; no headspace.

## Shipping
- Ship on ice or with cold packs.
- Include chain of custody form (CoC).
- Do NOT ship on Fridays (samples will arrive over weekend and may exceed hold time).
- Ship to Green Lab Receiving, 1200 Lab Way, Baltimore MD 21201.

## Acceptance / rejection criteria
- Sample rejected if:
  - Hold time exceeded on receipt
  - Container is glass (PFAS leaches from glass)
  - Temperature > 10 °C on receipt
  - No CoC

## Turnaround times
- Standard: 10 business days
- Rush (5 business days): +35% surcharge
- Stat (3 business days): +100% surcharge (subject to capacity)

## Method references
- EPA Method 537.1 (rev 2020)
- ISO 21675:2019
