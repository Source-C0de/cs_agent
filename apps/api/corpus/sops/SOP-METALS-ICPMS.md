# SOP-METALS-002 — Metals by ICP-MS (EPA 6020B)

Owner: Dr. Marcus Reyes
Effective: 2025-09-01

## Scope
ICP-MS determination of trace metals in water, soil, and biological matrices
using EPA Method 6020B.

## Container & preservation by matrix
| Matrix    | Container          | Preservation       |
|-----------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Groundwater | 500 mL HDPE      | HNO3 to pH < 2     |
| Drinking water | 500 mL HDPE | HNO3 to pH < 2     |
| Soil       | 4 oz glass jar    | Cool ≤ 6 °C        |
| Serum      | 7 mL royal blue top SST | None; spin and aliquot |

## Holding time
- Aqueous: 6 months from collection (per EPA 200.2).
- Soil: 6 months for most analytes; mercury 28 days.

## Sample volume
- Aqueous: minimum 250 mL; recommended 500 mL for low-level work.
- Soil: 10 g minimum wet weight.

## Analytes & reporting limits
Default panel includes: As, Ba, Be, Cd, Co, Cr, Cu, Mn, Mo, Ni, Pb, Sb, Se, Tl, V, Zn.
Reporting limits vary by matrix — contact the lab for project-specific LOQs.

## Turnaround times
- Standard: 7 business days
- Rush (4 business days): +50%
- Stat (2 business days): subject to instrument availability

## Interferences
- Polyatomic interferences (e.g. 40Ar16O on 56Fe) mitigated by collision cell (He mode).
- Memory effects from Hg mitigated by extended wash between samples.
